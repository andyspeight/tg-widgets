What you're looking at

Sliding digits
When a digit changes, the old number slides up and out, the new one slides up into place. Departure-board energy, no flip-card cliché.
Gradient cells
Each digit cell uses a brand-tinted vertical gradient instead of a flat colour. Subtle on its own, premium-feeling in aggregate.
Brand glow
A soft, brand-coloured shadow under each cell. Adds depth without becoming neon.
Final-minute ring
When ≤60 seconds remain, an SVG progress ring traces around the seconds cell, draining as time runs out. Click "Final minute" above to see it.
Hero aurora
A slow-moving brand-coloured radial gradient behind the Hero layout only. ~18s cycle, low opacity, fully respects reduced-motion preference.
Banner dot
A pulsing dot next to the Banner heading — the universal "live, in progress" signal. Live tickers everywhere use it.
