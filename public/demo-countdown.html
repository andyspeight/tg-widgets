Heads-up on client time: The widget uses the viewer's device clock. The configured target is stored as a fixed UTC instant, so the moment is unambiguous worldwide — but if a viewer's clock is wildly wrong, their countdown will be wrong too. That's fine for marketing campaigns. For legally binding deadlines, server-rendered timestamps would be the safer pattern.
